Honest, Unbiased Financial Planning

Mountainview Planning is an advice-only, fee-for-service financial planning firm based in Vancouver, BC. We provide expert, unbiased guidance to help you navigate your financial life with confidence. We don't sell any products or receive any commissions, so our advice is always 100% focused on your best interests.

Tired of Financial 'Advice' That Feels More Like a Sales Pitch?

You're not alone.

Many "financial advisors" at banks or large firms are actually salespeople. They are often paid commissions to steer you into specific mutual funds or insurance products, whether they're the best fit for you or not. 

This creates a massive conflict of interest. How can you be sure the advice you're getting is truly for you, or for their sales quota?

We do things differently.

What is Advice-Only Financial Planning?

Advice-only planning is a simple, transparent model that removes this conflict of interest.

As an advice-only planner, my only product is my professional expertise. You pay a clear, flat fee directly for my time and the comprehensive financial plan we build together.

That's it. No commissions. No selling you products. No third-party kickbacks. No hidden fees.

As a fiduciary, I am legally and ethically bound to act in your best interest, 100% of the time. My success is tied directly to your success, not to what I can sell you.
