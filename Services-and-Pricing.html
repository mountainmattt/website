The Clarity of 
Advice-Only Planning

I firmly believe that financial planning is a holistic process; and all of the pieces of your overall financial health are interconnected and dependent upon each other. For that reason, I've designed my comprehensive package to give you full confidence and understanding in where you are at, and more importantly, where you are going with your financial situation.

As an advice only fee-for-service financial planner, I offer fixed priced packages that depend upon the complexity of your financial situation. All prices are agreed upon beforehand. I do not engage with a client if I don't believe that my services can bring value into their lives that far outweigh the fees.

Our Collaborative Process

A meticulous six-phase journey to absolute financial clarity.

Phase 1
Inventory
Gathering your financial vitals and organizing your data to create a clear picture of where you stand today.

Phase 2
Discovery
An intentional conversation to define your ideal future, uncover challenges, and align your money with your values.

Phase 3
Create
I dive into the numbers, stress-testing different scenarios to build a personalized blueprint for your goals.

Phase 4
Present
A dedicated session to walk through your plan, explore the results, and ensure every question is answered

Phase 5
Handover
Access your final interactive plan and custom video walkthroughs and reports.

Phase 6
Implement
Transitioning from planning to doing. I provide the specific action steps or ongoing support to bring your plan to life.
