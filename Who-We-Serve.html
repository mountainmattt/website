Who We Serve

At Mountainview Financial Planning, I work with Canadians who value objective, conflict-free advice. Whether you are still climbing the professional ladder or preparing to step away from the workforce, my fee-only model ensures that the guidance you receive is focused entirely on your best interests, independent of product sales or assets under management fees.

Early & Mid Career Professionals

For those in their peak earning years, life is often a whirlwind of competing financial priorities. You are managing a growing career, perhaps raising a family, and trying to make sense of a complex Canadian financial landscape. Whether you are aiming for traditional retirement or pursuing a more accelerated path like Financial Independence, Retire Early (FIRE), the goal is to move beyond reactionary financial choices and transition into a proactive strategy. I help you build a cohesive framework that coordinates your income, debt, and savings goals so you can stop wondering if you are doing the right things and start moving forward with confidence.

My role is to act as a professional sounding board to help you filter out the noise. We focus on creating a resilient financial foundation that allows you to enjoy your current lifestyle while systematically building toward the work-optional life you envision for the future.

A Defined Roadmap: Creating a solid plan so you know exactly what you need to do to hit your major milestones, from home purchases to long-term wealth goals.

Tax Efficiency: Identifying the right balance between RRSP, TFSA, and FHSA contributions based on your current and future tax brackets.

Family Financial Milestones: Planning for the high costs of education through RESPs and ensuring your family is protected with proper insurance coverage.

Mortgage and Debt Strategy: Navigating interest rate environments and deciding how to prioritize debt repayment alongside long-term investing.

Career Transitions: Evaluating the financial impact of job changes, bonuses, or professional incorporation for business owners.

Pre & Post Retirees

The transition into retirement is one of the most significant shifts you will ever make. Most people at this stage are haunted by two fundamental questions: "When can I retire?" and "Will I have enough?" I help you answer these with certainty by replacing guesswork with a clear, sustainable roadmap. We look past day-to-day market fluctuations to focus on the bigger picture, ensuring your assets are structured to support your goals for as long as you need them.

Success in retirement requires a delicate balance. It is not just about paying the least amount of tax this year; it is about optimizing the interplay between your total lifetime spending, your lifetime tax bill, and the eventual after-tax estate you leave behind. Through rigorous stress testing, we evaluate how your plan holds up against inflation, market volatility, and changing health needs, giving you the confidence to spend your hard-earned money without the fear of running out.

Retirement Readiness: Determining your ideal retirement date based on a deep dive into your projected spending and income sources.

Strategic Decumulation: Coordinating the drawdown of RRSPs, RRIFs, and non-registered accounts to balance current income needs with future tax liabilities.

Government Benefit Timing: Analyzing the math behind CPP and OAS start dates to maximize your guaranteed, inflation-indexed floor.

Estate and Legacy Optimization: Planning for the efficient transfer of wealth to the next generation while minimizing probate and terminal tax bills.

Health and Contingency Planning: Factoring in the potential costs of long-term care and healthcare to ensure your lifestyle remains protected regardless of what the future holds.
