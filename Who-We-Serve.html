Mountainview Financial Planning is privileged to be based in the city now known as Vancouver, on the unceded, ancestral territories of the $xʷməθkʷəy̓əm$ (Musqueam), $Sḵwx̱wú7mesh$ (Squamish), and səlilwətaɬ (Tsleil-Waututh) Nations.

The mountains that grace our view and give our company its name are sacred to these Nations and hold stories that far predate colonial settlement. As a company focused on planning for the future, we are committed to reconciliation. This includes our own learning journey and reflecting on how we can contribute to a more equitable future for all. We invite our clients and visitors, wherever they may be, to learn about the Indigenous Peoples and traditional territories of the lands they call home.
